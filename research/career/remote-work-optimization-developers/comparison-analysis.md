```markdown
Cons:

- May need early morning availability
- Competition with local talent
- Less "follow the sun" advantage

- Afternoon/evening availability manageable
- Weekend coverage possible

- Late-night emergency support
- Limited family time in evenings
- Fatigue from extended hours

US (GMT-5 to -8):
Pros:
- 24-hour development cycle advantage
- Premium pricing for timezone coverage
- Less competition from other regions
- Exclusive night shift positioning

Cons:
- Significant personal life disruption
- Late night/early morning meetings
- Social isolation during work hours
- Health impacts from irregular schedule
```
