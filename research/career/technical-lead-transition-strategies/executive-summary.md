## Risk Mitigation

### ‚ö†Ô∏è Common Pitfalls and Solutions

**Cultural Misalignment**
- *Risk*: Philippines' high-context communication clashing with Western direct communication
- *Solution*: Invest in cross-cultural communication training and practice direct feedback delivery

**Timezone Management**
- *Risk*: Burnout from accommodating multiple timezone requirements
- *Solution*: Establish clear core hours and async communication protocols

**Remote Team Building**
- *Risk*: Difficulty building team cohesion without physical presence
- *Solution*: Invest in virtual team building tools and regular video check-ins

**Technical vs. People Balance**
- *Risk*: Spending too much time on technical work instead of leadership
- *Solution*: Set strict time boundaries (70% leadership, 30% technical maximum)
