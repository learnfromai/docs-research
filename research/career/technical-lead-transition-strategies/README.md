### Technical Leadership Transition Framework

| Transition Phase | Duration | Focus Areas | Key Activities | Success Metrics |
|-----------------|----------|-------------|----------------|-----------------|
| **Pre-Transition** | 2-4 weeks | Preparation and Planning | Stakeholder mapping, skill assessment, relationship building | Readiness assessment, stakeholder buy-in |
| **Foundation** (Days 1-30) | 1 month | Discovery and Relationship Building | Team assessment, process understanding, initial improvements | Team acceptance, relationship quality |
