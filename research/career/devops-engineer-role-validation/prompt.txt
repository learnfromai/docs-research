[Research]  DevOps Engineer Role Validation - Market demand and skill requirements analysis

### Research Guidelines & Requirements

This research focuses on validating the DevOps Engineer role in the current job market, analyzing:
- Market demand and hiring trends
- Essential vs. nice-to-have skills
- Salary ranges and compensation
- Career progression opportunities
- Industry certifications and learning paths
- Geographic and company size variations
- Remote work opportunities and trends
