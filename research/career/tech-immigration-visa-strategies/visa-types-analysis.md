### Temporary Work Visas

**Temporary Skill Shortage (TSS) visa (subclass 482)**

Short-term Stream (2 years):
‚ñ° Occupation on Short-term Skilled Occupation List (STSOL)
‚ñ° English requirement: IELTS 5.0 overall, 4.5 each band
‚ñ° Work experience: 2 years in nominated occupation
‚ñ° No pathway to permanent residency

‚ñ° Occupation on Medium and Long-term Strategic Skills List (MLTSSL)

‚ñ° Work experience: 2 years in nominated occupation
‚ñ° Pathway to ENS 186 after 3 years

```

‚ñ° Exceptional talent in one of 10 target sectors (including Digital Technologies)
‚ñ° No sponsorship required - can be self-nominated
‚ñ° Prominent person in field must support application
‚ñ° Ability to attract premium salary (AUD $162,000+ or equivalent)
‚ñ° Health and character requirements
‚ñ° Processing time: 6-10 months
‚ñ° Cost: AUD $4,045 main applicant

### Permanent Residence Visas

**Skilled Independent visa (subclass 189)**
```
Points-Based System Requirements:
‚ñ° Occupation on Medium and Long-term Strategic Skills List
‚ñ° Positive skills assessment from relevant authority
‚ñ° Points test score minimum 65 (practically 85-95 for ICT)
‚ñ° English proficiency: IELTS 6.0 overall, 6.0 each band (minimum)
‚ñ° Age limit: Under 45 years at time of invitation
‚ñ° Health and character requirements
‚ñ° No sponsorship required
‚ñ° Processing time: 8-11 months
‚ñ° Cost: AUD $4,045 main applicant
```

**Employer Nomination Scheme (ENS) visa (subclass 186)**
```
Direct Entry Stream:
‚ñ° Never held TSS 482 visa
‚ñ° Skills assessment required
‚ñ° English requirement: IELTS 6.0 overall, 5.0 each band minimum
‚ñ° Work experience: 3 years in nominated occupation
‚ñ° Employer sponsorship required
‚ñ° Processing time: 6-8 months
‚ñ° Cost: AUD $4,045 main applicant

Temporary Residence Transition Stream:
‚ñ° Hold TSS 482 for minimum 3 years
‚ñ° Work for nominating employer for 3 years
‚ñ° Skills assessment may not be required
‚ñ° English requirement: IELTS 6.0 overall, 5.0 each band
‚ñ° Processing time: 6-8 months
‚ñ° Cost: AUD $4,045 main applicant
```

### Work Visas

**Global Talent visa**
```
Exceptional Talent Route:
‚ñ° Endorsement from Tech Nation required
‚ñ° Evidence of exceptional ability in digital technology
‚ñ° International recognition and significant contributions
‚ñ° No salary requirement or job offer needed

‚ñ° Processing time: Up to 3 months

## üá∫üá∏ United States - Visa Categories Analysis

### Temporary Work Visas

**H-1B Specialty Occupation**
```
Lottery System Requirements:
‚ñ° Bachelor's degree or equivalent in specialty occupation
‚ñ° Job offer from US employer willing to sponsor
‚ñ° Occupation requires theoretical and practical application of specialized knowledge
‚ñ° Employer must file Labor Condition Application (LCA)
‚ñ° Annual cap: 65,000 regular + 20,000 advanced degree exemption
‚ñ° Lottery registration period: March (exact dates vary yearly)
‚ñ° Petition filing: April 1 - June 30 (if selected)
‚ñ° Processing time: 2-6 months standard, 15 days premium
‚ñ° Cost: $460 base + $4,000 training fee + $500 fraud fee
‚ñ° Duration: 3 years initial, renewable for additional 3 years
```

**O-1 Extraordinary Ability**
```
Evidence Requirements (3 of 8 criteria):
‚ñ° Awards or prizes for excellence in field
‚ñ° Membership in associations requiring outstanding achievements
‚ñ° Published material about applicant in professional publications
‚ñ° Participation as judge of work of others
‚ñ° Original contributions of major significance
‚ñ° Authorship of scholarly articles
‚ñ° Critical capacity employment for distinguished organizations
‚ñ° High salary or remuneration

No Annual Cap Limitations:
‚ñ° Can be filed at any time throughout the year
‚ñ° Premium processing available for 15-day processing
‚ñ° Easier to extend and transfer between employers
‚ñ° Strong foundation for EB-1 Green Card application
‚ñ° Processing time: 2-4 months standard, 15 days premium
‚ñ° Cost: $460 base + $500 fraud fee + $2,500 premium (optional)
‚ñ° Duration: Up to 3 years initial, renewable in 1-year increments
```

**L-1 Intracompany Transfer**
```
L-1A Executive/Managerial:
‚ñ° Must have worked for qualifying organization abroad for 1 year out of past 3
‚ñ° Must be coming to work in executive or managerial capacity
‚ñ° US and foreign companies must have qualifying relationship
‚ñ° Duration: Up to 7 years total
‚ñ° Dual intent allowed - can apply for Green Card

L-1B Specialized Knowledge:
‚ñ° Must have specialized knowledge of company's products, services, or procedures
‚ñ° Same employment history and company relationship requirements
‚ñ° Duration: Up to 5 years total
‚ñ° Processing time: 2-4 months standard, 15 days premium
‚ñ° Cost: $460 base + $500 fraud fee + $2,500 premium (optional)
```

### Permanent Residence (Green Card) Categories

**EB-1 Priority Workers**
```
EB-1A Extraordinary Ability:
‚ñ° Same criteria as O-1 visa but for permanent residency
‚ñ° No labor certification required
‚ñ° Self-petitioning allowed
‚ñ° Processing time: 8-12 months
‚ñ° No per-country limits for Philippines (currently)

EB-1B Outstanding Professors and Researchers:
‚ñ° International recognition for outstanding achievements
‚ñ° At least 3 years teaching or research experience
‚ñ° Job offer for tenured or tenure-track position
‚ñ° Processing time: 8-12 months

EB-1C Multinational Executives and Managers:
‚ñ° Worked for qualifying organization abroad for 1 year out of past 3
‚ñ° Coming to work in executive or managerial capacity
‚ñ° US and foreign companies must have qualifying relationship
‚ñ° Processing time: 8-12 months
```

**EB-2 Advanced Degree Professionals**
```
EB-2 with Labor Certification (PERM):
‚ñ° Master's degree or bachelor's + 5 years progressive experience
‚ñ° Labor certification required (PERM process)
‚ñ° Job offer and employer sponsorship required
‚ñ° Total processing time: 2-4 years including PERM
‚ñ° Current priority dates for Philippines

EB-2 National Interest Waiver (NIW):
‚ñ° Advanced degree or exceptional ability
‚ñ° Work must be in national interest of United States
‚ñ° Self-petitioning allowed - no employer sponsorship required
‚ñ° No labor certification required
‚ñ° Processing time: 12-18 months
```

**EB-3 Skilled Workers**
```
Professional Category:
‚ñ° Bachelor's degree required
‚ñ° Job offer and labor certification required
‚ñ° Lower priority than EB-1 and EB-2
‚ñ° Processing time: 2-5 years including PERM
‚ñ° Cost: Similar to EB-2 process
```

**EB-5 Investor**
```
Investment Requirements:
‚ñ° $800,000 investment in Targeted Employment Area
‚ñ° $1,050,000 investment in high unemployment area
‚ñ° Create or preserve 10 full-time jobs for US workers
‚ñ° Two-year conditional permanent residency initially
‚ñ° Processing time: 3-5 years total including conditional period
‚ñ° Success rate: 85-90% for well-structured projects
```
